What We Do

Within the expansive realm of UPU Canada, our multifaceted engagement with the Urhobo community manifests through a rich tapestry of diverse activities and initiatives meticulously designed to offer unwavering support. Delving into the tapestry of our services reveals a vibrant array of offerings, ranging from culturally immersive events that celebrate the rich heritage of the Urhobo people to thoughtfully crafted educational programs aimed at empowering and enlightening our community members. In our relentless pursuit of fostering community cohesion, we extend our reach through dynamic community outreach efforts, ensuring that every member of the Urhobo diaspora in Canada feels not only acknowledged but cherished.

Advocacy, as a cornerstone of our mission, propels us forward in championing the needs and interests of the Urhobo community, acting as a steadfast voice that echoes through the corridors of influence. Our overarching goal, a beacon guiding our every endeavor, is to meticulously construct a profound sense of belonging within the Urhobo community, transcending geographical boundaries to create a nurturing environment that encourages growth, resilience, and prosperity for all our cherished community members navigating the Canadian landscape. In this spirit, we passionately provide resources—both tangible and intangible—that serve as pillars supporting the aspirations and dreams of our community, enabling them not just to survive but to truly thrive in the mosaic of Canadian society.
